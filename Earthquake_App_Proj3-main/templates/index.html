The Alert system for earthquakes is the Prompt Assessment of Global Earthquakes for Response (PAGER) which grades earthquakes based off a color gradient. Each color represents the threat level of the earthquake as follows:
  • Green: Estimated Fatalities: 0 , Estimated Losses: < $1 million
  • Yellow: Estimated Fatalities: 1-99 , Estimated Losses: $1 million-$100 million
  • Orange: Estimated Fatalities: 100-999 , Estimated Losses: $100 million-$1 billion
  • Red: Estimated Fatalities: 1000+ , Estimated Losses: $1 billion+
